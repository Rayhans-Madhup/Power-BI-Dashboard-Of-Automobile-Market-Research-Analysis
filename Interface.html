Business-Related Questions & Answers

1. What is the purpose of this Power BI Dashboard?

The dashboard aims to provide insights into the automobile market using data visualization. It helps stakeholders understand market trends, consumer preferences, and product performance.

2. How can businesses benefit from this dashboard?

Businesses can use it to make data-driven decisions about inventory management, marketing strategies, and customer targeting. It enhances visibility into sales trends and market dynamics.

3. Which KPIs are essential in automobile market analysis?

Key KPIs include Total Sales, Market Share by Brand, Regional Sales Distribution, Growth Rate, Average Selling Price, and Customer Segmentation.

4. How does this dashboard help in competitive analysis?

By comparing different manufacturers and models, the dashboard identifies strong and weak performers, helping businesses benchmark against competitors.

5. Can this dashboard support strategic planning?

Yes. It offers a holistic view of the market, which aids in planning product launches, pricing strategies, and entry into new regions or segments.
